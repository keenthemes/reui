ReUI, as a first-class shadcn registry, is evolving to contribute meaningfully to the shadcn ecosystem.

Our focus is on providing an exceptional [component library](/docs) and [catalog](/components) that are both
production-ready and AI-ready, empowering developers to ship shadcn projects faster.

## Current Status

- **ReUI 2.0 release**
- **[shadcn/create](/docs/shadcn-create) integration**
- **10+ new components**
- **1000+ registry components**

## What's Next

- Expand the **Components** set with new unique components.
- Grow the **component catalog** for more real-world use cases.
- Launch **ReUI Pro** with a shadcn Figma kit, exclusive blocks, templates, and full-stack boilerplates.
